Services & Features

OFFICE SPACE OPTIONS

PRIVATE CABIN

A Private Office Membership is a fully enclosed space built for a team of 1-10 people (or more) who demand a lot of concentration and privacy. They are the perfect business solution for individual, start-up, freelancer and SME’s. Private cabins are ideal for small corporate teams to work as a unit, increase efficiency and conduct operations from the same space. This space is for those that require a private, quiet space to conduct intensive operations that require a lot of concentration and confidentiality. The office will be furnished with tables, chairs and a locker. Printing, WiFi, and package collection services are provided for free. Additionally, complimentary use of our conference room with reservation.

DEDICATED DESK

Dedicated Desks are the perfect business solution for the solo entrepreneur, start-ups and freelancers. A singular unit where you will get office furniture & locker enclosed to go with your Dedicated table. You will even have printing, Wi-Fi and package services free. Additionally, complimentary use of our Conference Room with a reservation. This set up still permits for essential collaboration, whereas still having your own house - only for you.

FLEXI WORK STATIONS

Flexible work stations are the latest trend in business and we are all about keeping up with the best. It’s a step away from the ‘old-office’ workplace style of cubicles and solitary workspaces, in favor of associated, unrestricted and cooperative workplace style.Versatility, portability and working on-the-go has never been easier!Workstations positioned in an unrestricted layout encourages communication and promotes cross industry learning. Our flexiwork space is intended to supply a thought provoking and co-productive environment for our members. Whether you’d like to go solo or toss some concepts around with others, the choice is yours.

VIRTUAL OFFICE

A virtual workplace is a workplace that is not located in any one physical space; rather, several workplaces are technologically connected. Virtual workplace memberships include a prestigious back bay business address, mail handling, company registration, telephone handling and storage options.
