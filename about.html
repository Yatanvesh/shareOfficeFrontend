About Us

About SOS

India, being a country of 135 crore citizens, it is not surprising that we are considered a hub for diversity, innovation and creation. We, as a country have become the biggest investment opportunity in terms of start ups in the world. And so, it is only fair that we support this movement by encouraging entrepreneurs and their teams by providing them the absolute best working conditions in order to grow together and achieve success without putting a major dent in their financial position. From one start up to another, we understand the difficulties and challenges that you may face, which means we will go that extra mile to try and give you all the support you may possibly need. Being a part of the SOS community doesn’t just mean coming in to work through the week but also looking to collaborate with others in the community in order to be bigger and better together. And that’s what Share Office Solutions is all about.




Mission

To create, collaborate, encourage and enhance a responsible, sustainable and cooperative business environment.

Vision

To pioneer a state-of-the-art incubation centre for all industry professionals to grow together, overcome any challenges and succeed preparedly for today and tomorrow.
